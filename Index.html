Science Objectives for Everyone

The High Definition Earth Viewing (HDEV) experiment places four commercially available HD cameras on the exterior of the space station and uses them to stream live video of Earth for viewing online. The cameras are enclosed in a temperature specific housing and are exposed to the harsh radiation of space. Analysis of the effect of space on the video quality, over the time HDEV is operational, may help engineers decide which cameras are the best types to use on future missions.

NASA-Johnson and the University of Houston-Clear Lake have collaborated on this project and are working together with students from the University of Hamburg to control the cameras.
